Creme Brulee


Description

The simpliest Creme Brulee Recipe for anyone to follow. Rice and creamy with just the right jiggliness all in one. Finished with the best crack of crispy sugar for the perfect crisp to creamy ratio.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Whisk together 1 cup heavy cream and 3 tablespoons white sugar in microwave-safe bowl until combined
  3. Cook in microwave until warm. About 1-2 mins, whisk again to dissolve sugar
  4. Whisk in 3 egg yolks and 1/4 teaspoon vanilla until smooth
  5. Place 2 ramekins into a roasting pan
  6. Pour cream mixture into the 2 ramekins
  7. Pour enough hot water into roasting pan, about half the ramekins
  8. Bake for 50 mins or until custard is set but slightly jiggly in the center when shaken
  9. Remove ramekins from roasting pan and chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours or until cold
  10. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon white sugar evenly over the top of each ramekin
  11. Using a kitch torch melt and lightly toast sugar on top until brown and bubbly
  12. Let sugar topping cool to harden
  13. Serve and Enjoy!
