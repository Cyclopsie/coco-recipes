Appetizer Meatballs

Description

This is the simpliest appetizer you will make! With only three ingredients to make the sweet and juicy meatballs. It will be everyone's favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage
  • 1 pound ground spicy pork sausage
  • 1(6 ounces) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine pork sausage and spicy pork sausage
  2. Form mixture into 1/2 inch balls, about 1 tablespoon each
  3. Heat oil in a skillet
  4. Fry meatballs until browned and completly cooked through, about 15 to 20 minutes. If using thermometer, center of meatball should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C)
  5. Transfer meatballs to a large bowl
  6. Add half of the orange juice concentrate, gently toss to coat meatballs
  7. Put meatballs in a slow cooker
  8. Add remainging orange juice concentrate
  9. Turn slow cooker to low and keep warm
  10. Serve and Enjoy!
