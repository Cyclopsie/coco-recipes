Sloppy Joes

Description

This Sloppy Joe is thick and juicy. These are meant to be messy and eaten with your hands. Served with hamburger buns.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups water, divided
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat up a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Place ground beef and onions on skillet
  3. Cook and stir until beef is borwned and crumbled, about 10 mins
  4. Stir in green peppers and garlic, until softened, 2-3 minutes
  5. Add 1 cup water and stir, scraping pan ocassionally
  6. Stir in ketchup, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, salt, black pepper, and Worcestershire sauce
  7. Add remaingin 1 cup water and bring to boil
  8. Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until liquid has evaporated and mixture is thick, about 40 mins
  9. Season with salt, black pepper, Worcestershire sauce and cayenne pepper
  10. Serve over toasted hamburger buns and enjoy!
